Intrastate Crowdfunding; The Present and the Future

Currently, only three States in the Union (Georgia, Kansas and Wisconsin) have fully legalized securities based crowdfunding that permits general solicitation to the general public, both accredited and non-accredited investors. If you live in one of these States, you can legally crowdfund by offering securities (equity or debt, for example), right now! Other States are also considering legalizing intrastate crowdfunding (WI, NC, WA and NJ), and more will follow.

Most folks have heard about national crowdfunding under Title III of the JOBS Act, while very few have heard about purely “intrastate” crowdfunding. This is going to change. Title III of the JOBS Act is expensive, cumbersome, and not yet in force (perhaps 3rd quarter of 2014). Intrastate crowdfunding is already here, and is only growing in popularity across the country.
