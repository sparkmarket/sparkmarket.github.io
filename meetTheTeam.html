Meet The Team

Megan Johnson, Founder & CEO

Megan is a Georgia attorney with six years of experience with financial transactions and general business law. She is dedicated to creating a new marketplace for quality and sustainable local investment opportunities accessible by everyday Georgia residents.

Jeff Bekiares, Founder & COO

Jeff is a Georgia attorney with over six years of experience in securities and corporate law. He was one of the first attorneys to identify the crowdfunding potential of the Invest Georgia Exemption ("IGE") and has worked closely with regulators and experts in the state and national crowdfunding community to design IGE campaigns that are compliant and safe for issuers and investors.

Marcus Cannady, VP / Marketing & Development

Among his SparkMarket peers, Marcus is the resident non-attorney; however, before having warm feelings, he spent nearly 10 years working in the banking industry. In fact, it is his experience in mortgage finance, retail banking and bank management consulting that eventually led to “Big Bank” fatigue. His refuge was found working within Atlanta’s thriving advertising/marketing industry with Cox Media Group. SparkMarket has allowed for the marriage of all these skill sets and experiences--he couldn’t be happier.

Luan Cox, Advisor

Luan is the Founder and CEO of Crowdnetic. Luan has deep financial data and applications experience and has previously served as the General Manager and Vice President of Interactive Data Managed Solutions - Americas (acquired by Silver Lake and Warburg Pincus), Executive Vice President of Global Sales and Marketing for Stockpoint (acquired by ScreamingMedia and later MarketWatch, now part of IDC), Executive Director of Partner Solutions for Quote.com (acquired by IDC). She is also a proud Board member of the Crowdfunding Professional Association (CfPA) and is a founding Council Member of the Crowdfunding Accreditation for Platform Standards (CAPS).

Dara Albright, Advisor

Dara serves as Crowdnetic's Chief Strategy Officer. She is a thought provoker and frequent speaker on topics relating to market structure, private secondary transactions and crowdfinance. Dara founded and authors the NowStreet Journal, the primary provider of analysis and insight into the crowdfinance industry as well as the legislation and innovation currently fueling it. She is also the co-founder of LendIt, the preeminent conference organization dedicated exclusively to the burgeoning P2P and online lending industry. Dara has held a distinguished 21 year career in IPO execution, investment banking, corporate communications, financial marketing and institutional equity sales. She is a visionary who continues to introduce neoteric asset classes and ground-breaking financial products to the Wall Street community. Prior firms she worked for include: Unterberg Towbin, Morgan Stanley Dean Witter, Divine Capital and Citigate Dewe Rogerson. Dara is a co-founder of the Crowdfund Intermediary Regulatory Advocates (CFIRA) and the Crowdfunding Professional Association (CfPA). She is a graduate of the George Washington University and holds securities industry Series 7, 24, 31 and 63 licenses.
