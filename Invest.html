  • How do I Invest?

    Basic Information

    You will be directed to submit certain information to confirm you are a Georgia resident and otherwise qualified to invest in the offering

    Approval Process

    We will review your materials and let you know when you are approved and ready to initiate the payment process through our trusted partner BancBox.

    BancBox Process

    BancBox Crowd is a money transfer platform that will be handling and processing all of the money transfers and escrow accounts for investors and businesses on SparkMarket. To invest on SparkMarket, you will open up a free BancBox account, which you will fund through SparkMarket's payment gateway prior to investing. For more information, visit BancBox.

    Offering Documents

    You will have an opportunity to review the offering documents off-line and ask questions of the company management team, review the business plan and find out more information about the company.

    Fund Your Account

    Once you have been approved, you will be directed to SparkMarket’s payment gateway to fund your investor account and authorize the transfer of funds into the BancBox escrow account.

    Investment is Final

    When the offering reaches its minimum target amount and the deal closes, the offering proceeds will be transferred to the company’s BancBox account and then directed to an institution qualified to do business in the state of Georgia. If the campaign fails to reach its equity funding target, your investment will be returned to you at no charge.

    Closing Documents

    Once the deal is closed, you will receive a copy of the company’s organizational materials, the subscription agreement and a certificate of ownership.
